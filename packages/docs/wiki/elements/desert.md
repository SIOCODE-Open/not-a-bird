# Desert

![](../images/item.desert.png)

**Tier**: 1

A vast sandbox of banishment where even cacti consider sunblock.

## How to make?

* Combine [Earth](/wiki/elements/earth) and [Beach](/wiki/elements/beach) to make [Desert](/wiki/elements/desert). This process is known as [Make Desert](/wiki/recipes/make-desert).

## How to use?

* Combine [Water](/wiki/elements/water) and [Desert](/wiki/elements/desert) to make [Oasis](/wiki/elements/oasis). This process is known as [Make Oasis](/wiki/recipes/make-oasis).
* Combine [Fire](/wiki/elements/fire) and [Desert](/wiki/elements/desert) to make [Mirage](/wiki/elements/mirage). This process is known as [Make Mirage](/wiki/recipes/make-mirage).
* Combine [Wind](/wiki/elements/wind) and [Desert](/wiki/elements/desert) to make [Sand Dune](/wiki/elements/sand-dune). This process is known as [Make Sand Dune](/wiki/recipes/make-sand-dune).
