# Marmalade

![](/wiki/images/item.marmalade.png)

**Tier**: 1

The sticky, fruity concoction that Paddington Bear would trade his hat for.

## How to make?

_There is no way to make Marmalade. You can buy it from the game, by pressing the **Buy** button._
